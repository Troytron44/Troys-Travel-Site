About

Hello! My name is Troy and this is my family. We enjoy traveling and have made it an effort and a commitment to get out and see new things together. We wanted to share some of our trips and adventures with you. Traveling with a little one can have its challenges but it's not impossible! Some of the places we have visited are extremely fun for families. From the Corpus Christi Beach, to Disney World in Florida, and all the way to Brazil! We found that as long as you have a plan and give yourself time, it can be stress free. We enjoy sharing and hopefully, our experiences inspire you to get out and make memories as a family!
